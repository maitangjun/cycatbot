The Follow MI Smart Pet Ecosystem, Follow MI —— is "Follow Me", also "Follow 咪". Technology follows, and love responds.

It's not just a complete set of devices, but also an online cat health manager and interaction expert:
Real-time health monitoring — through collar and litter box, continuously tracking physiological data such as heart rate, weight, and urinary indicators;
Panoramic home care — check cat status anytime, support real-time voice intercom and mobile live streaming, locate at any time;
Active fun interaction — when the system determines the cat is bored, BOT will project electronic mice on the ground, can also play animations, and can video connect with the cat owner in case of emergency.
