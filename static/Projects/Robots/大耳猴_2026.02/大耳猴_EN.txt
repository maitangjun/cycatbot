**Project Number**: 13

Cheburashka - A mysterious cute creature that emerged from a tropical orange box, named for its frequent falls. It accompanies a crocodile, heals loneliness with kindness, and is a national cultural treasure of Russia.

1. Birth Background
Literary Father: First created in 1966 by Soviet writer Eduard Uspensky in the picture book "Crocodile Gena and His Friends".
Animation Peak: In 1969, it was made into stop-motion animation by Soyuzmultfilm studio, and its melancholy yet warm image was officially established.

2. Unique Character Traits
Extreme Kindness: There are no real enemies in its worldview. Even when facing the mischievous old lady Shapoklyak, it always responds with politeness and tolerance.
"Outsider" Resonance: It has no identity proof and doesn't belong to any known species. This theme of "searching for belonging" resonates deeply not only with children but also with many adults.

3. Cultural Influence
Fashion and Sports: Since the 2004 Athens Olympics, it has worn different colored national team sportswear multiple times, becoming the cute spokesperson for Russian sports spirit.
