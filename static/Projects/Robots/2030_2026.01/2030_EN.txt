By 2030, household robots have already taken initial shape.

The concept of 2030 household robots is not only to perform basic housework and companionship, but also to transform into furniture in the space during idle time or charging.

Currently, two small robots that can become tables and lamps have been created, respectively adapting to minimalist Japanese family style and Spanish style homes. In the future, it is hoped that robots can simultaneously meet practical and family aesthetic needs.
