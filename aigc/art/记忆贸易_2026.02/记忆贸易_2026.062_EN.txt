When Algorithms Take Over Narrative, Where Will Humanity's Last Private Property – Memory – Go?

"Memory Trade" is a series of interactive robotic installation art driven by AI. Placed within the gaps of high-density digital landscapes, this series reconstructs visual vocabulary through algorithmic logic. Within its generative and precise structure, it profoundly explores the complex symbiotic and parasitic relationship between the cornerstone of human emotion – "memory" – and alienated technology in the AI era.
